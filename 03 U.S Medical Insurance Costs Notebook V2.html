#Function to inject data into empty lists
def inject_data(lst, csv_file, column_name):
    with open(csv_file) as csv_info:
        csv_dict = csv.DictReader(csv_info)
        for row in csv_dict:
            lst.append(row[column_name])
        return lst

Population Analysis¶

The average age of an individual within the dataset is 39 years of age.

The average bmi of an individual within the data is 30.66.

Females account for 49.48% of the data set, whilst males account for 50.52%.

The average number of children for each adult in the data is 1.09.

20.48% of the data set are smokers.

The average cost for medical insurance in the US is $13,270.42.

Chart Analysis¶

As seen above we can see that the population is relatively spread evenly across each region, however the southeast does represent roughly 11% more of the dataset when compared to the other regions.

Furthermore, NHS bmi guidelines show that a large number of the dataset is either overweight or obese:

  • below 18.5 – the underweight range
  • between 18.5 and 24.9 – the healthy weight range
  • between 25 and 29.9 – the overweight range
  • between 30 and 39.9 – the obese range

Additionally we can also see that as age increases so does the average cost of insurance, by the time an individual reaches 60+ they can expect to pay roughly double the medical insurance they were paying at age 20.
